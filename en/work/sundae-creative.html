About

Montreal-based Sundae Creative is a communications agency that rethinks traditional strategies to maximize brand impact. In 2023, it joined forces with Groupe Dulcedo, reinforcing its expansion and innovative approach. Together, we reimagined their visual identity, blending bold editorial layouts, a sophisticated mix of serif and sans-serif fonts, and a playful color palette inspired by chocolate, ice cream, and cherry.

Our goal? A site that feels both refined and fun, with engaging animations and seamless transitions to elevate the user experience. It was a true creative partnership, pushing boundaries to deliver something unique.
